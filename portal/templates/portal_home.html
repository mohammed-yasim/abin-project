The novel coronavirus is a recently emerged human pathogen that has spread widely since December 2019.

    Our objective is to motivate the public people to use the web application to find their (self-assess) probability to be affected by the disease. And also, it helps to communicate with the community kitchen and Needhi medical shop to avail, needed food and medicines.

    We allows the users to check their probability of infection with automated and evidence-based self-assessment. Public users get their real-time evaluation by analyzing simple details such as users body temperature, body pain, breathing conditions, recent travel history and more
